Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?

▼

Most landing pages are completed within 1-2 weeks. For more complex multi-page websites or e-commerce platforms, the timeline usually ranges from 4-6 weeks depending on the scope and feedback speed.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

▼

Yes, I offer monthly maintenance packages that include security updates, regular backups, and small content tweaks to ensure your site stays performant and up-to-date.

What do I need to provide to get started?

▼

To start, I'll need your brand assets (logo, colors, fonts), any specific copy/text you want included, and high-resolution images. If you don't have these ready, I can assist with sourcing or strategy.

Will I be able to edit the website after it's finished?

▼

Absolutely. Every site I build is "Responsive by Design," meaning it will look and function perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop monitors alike.
